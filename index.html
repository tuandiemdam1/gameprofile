About

I am an R&D Engineer specialising in Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR) with deep expertise in control systems, navigation algorithms, and fleet management. I currently work at Viettel Post Corporation, where I lead end-to-end development of AGV/AMR logistics systems — from system architecture and mechanical design to firmware and control software.

My research background spans three laboratories at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, covering local path planning, multi-robot coordination, and industrial automation. I hold a B.Eng. in Control Engineering and Automation (GPA 3.42/4.0) and am a recipient of the Viettel Excellence Scholarship (2025) for top engineering talent.

I have authored multiple patents and software copyrights in AMR systems, including deep learning-based localisation, autonomous charging, and fleet coordination algorithms. When I'm not building robots, I enjoy reading about control theory and exploring new advances in AI and computer vision.
